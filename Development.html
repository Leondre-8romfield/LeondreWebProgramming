My Strengths

In regards to my Strengths: I am a critical and logical thinker, creative mind that explores innovation and I am also person who tries to be self aware.

In my reasoning of present occurances, I try to think realistically in achieving contemporary tasks. When I think of the future (whether near or distant) I explore my creativity to widen my scope. Lastly being self-aware I maintain personal integrity and strive not to be immoral or unjust.

Have I been improving on my skills? No, not yet atleast; But I have been trying my best to maintain my strengths.

Pillar 5

Don't put it off; the end of your life will arrive sooner than you think.
Remember that every day you spend is one fewer day you have to spend,
so make the most of each one. "Every day brings a plethora of chances,
but there is only one great opportunity."

Pillar 6

Every relationship you have, whether good, neutral, or bad,
is an association. Choose your friends carefully because you will become like them.
Surround yourself with winners, successful individuals who demonstrate
and exemplify principles and talents you want to learn and improve.

Pillar 7

After completing your formal education, you will spend the majority of your life.
Wealth, health, and spirituality all start with education.
Consider your personal experiences to see what went well and what didn't.
Read literature, look at successful individuals, and think about yourself.
You will make a fortune through self-education.
