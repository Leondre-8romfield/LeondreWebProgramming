Website Theme

The orange and cool black used on my pages were to stimulate a sense of professionalism,
and creativity. The orange on the home page was intented to be warm and inviting, whilst
alluding to my creative and somewhat artistic personality. On other pages the background
standard was the "cool black" color to deliver my content.
